Quick Reference Examples

hello→aGVsbG8=
Camp Cyber Academy→Q2FtcCBDeWJlciBBY2FkZW15
/secret→L3NlY3JldA==
/treasure→L3RyZWFzdXJl
/level2→L2xldmVsMg==
How Base64 works: Every 3 bytes of input → 4 Base64 characters. Characters used: A–Z, a–z, 0–9, +, / (64 total). The = is padding.

Important: Base64 is encoding, not encryption. Anyone can decode it instantly — it just makes text safe to embed anywhere. Real encryption (AES, RSA) requires a secret key to reverse.

Tip for Day 4: To hide a URL path like /secret in your puzzle, encode it here and paste the result into your HTML comment.
